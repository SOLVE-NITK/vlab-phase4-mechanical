Effect of infill density on tensile strength.

Objective:

To determine the effect of infill density on ultimate tensile strength.


Apparatus used:

3D printer, universal testing machine etc.

 Description Ultimate tensile strength is the maximum stress that a material can withstand while being stretched or pulled before breaking.

Infill Density is the amount of material filled inside the shells of the specimen. It is denoted in percentage value.
