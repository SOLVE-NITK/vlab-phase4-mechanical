FORGING

Objective:

To demonstrate the open die Forging operation.


Apparatus used:

Hydraulic press, Heater, workpiece, grinder etc.

STEP  1  Heat the workpiece to required temperature.

STEP  2  Set the hot workpiece in the forging machine.

STEP  3 Apply force on the workpiece to forge it.

STEP  4  Remove the forged workpiece and allow it to cool.

STEP  5  Do the finishing works.
