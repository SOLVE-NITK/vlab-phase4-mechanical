Forging

Objective:

To demonstrate the open die Forging operation.

Apparatus Used:

Hydraulic press, Heater, workpiece, grinder etc.

STEP 1Heat the workpiece to required temperature.

STEP 2Set the hot workpiece in the forging machine.

STEP 3Apply force on the workpiece to forge it.

STEP 4Remove the forged workpiece and allow it to cool.

STEP 5Do the finishing works.
