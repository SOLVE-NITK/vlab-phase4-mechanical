Brazing

Objective:

To join the given two work pieces as required by brazing.

Apparatus Used:

Mild steel rod, Filler rod, Flux, Protective equipments, Brazing torch, Grinder, Brush etc.

STEP 1Clean the given workpiece thoroughly,i.e. remove the rust, scales from the surface.

STEP 2Align the workpieces properly.

STEP 3Use a brush to apply the flux on the workpiece.

STEP 4Place the filler material at correct position.

STEP 5Heat the joint to the proper brazing temperature by using brazing torch.

STEP 6Clean the joint using a wire brush.
