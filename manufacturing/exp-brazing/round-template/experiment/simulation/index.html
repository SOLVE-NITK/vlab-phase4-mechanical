BRAZING

Objective:

To join the given two work pieces as required by brazing.


Apparatus used:

Mild steel rod, Filler rod, Flux, Protective equipments, Brazing torch, Grinder, Brush etc.

STEP  1 Clean the given workpiece thoroughly,i.e. remove the rust, scales from the surface.

-->

STEP  2  Align the workpieces properly.

STEP  3  Use a brush to apply the flux on the workpiece.

STEP  4 Place the filler material at correct position.

STEP  5  Heat the joint to the proper brazing temperature by using brazing torch.

STEP  6 Clean the joint using a wire brush.
