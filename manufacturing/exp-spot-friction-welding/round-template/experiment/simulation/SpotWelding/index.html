SPOT WELDING

Objective:

To prepare a Single Strap Butt Joint on the given work pieces using spot welding.


Apparatus used:

Wire Brush, Grinding Machine, Protective Equipments, Spot welding machine , Chipping Hammer, Mild steel workpiece etc.

STEP  1 Clean the given workpiece thoroughly, i.e. remove the rust, scales.

STEP  2  Position the metal workpieces one above another.

STEP  3  Turn ON the machine and adjust current.

STEP  4  Press the lever, so that the movable electrode moves towards the fixed electrode.A low voltage and very high current is passed through the joint for a very short time. The duration of the current flow is for about 2 sec. Then the metal under electrodes pressure is squeezed and welded.

STEP  5  Turn OFF the machine and take out joined workpieces from the machine.

STEP  6  Clean the workpiece using wire brush.
