Arc Welding (Lap Joint)

Objective:

To Prepare a lap joint using arc welding process.

Apparatus Used:

Wire Brush, Grinding Machine, Protective Equipments, Welding Transformer, Electrodes, Tong, Chipping Hammer, Mild steel flat of 60x45x10mm etc.

STEP 1Clean the given workpiece thoroughly, i.e. remove the rust, scales and form v cutting in the edges.

STEP 2Position the workpieces to form a lap joint.

STEP 3Adjust the current in machine.

STEP 4Strike the arc and move the electrode to the other end uniformly.

STEP 5 Clean the weld with a hammer and wire brush.
