ARC WELDING(LAP JOINT)

Objective:

To Prepare a lap joint using arc welding process.


Apparatus used:

Wire Brush, Grinding Machine, Protective Equipments, Welding Transformer, Electrodes, Tong, Chipping Hammer, Mild steel flat of 60x45x10mm etc.

STEP  1 Clean the given workpiece thoroughly, i.e. remove the rust, scales and file the edges.

STEP  2  Position the workpieces to form a lap joint.

STEP  3  Adjust the current in machine .

STEP  4   Strike the arc and move the electrode to the other end uniformly.

STEP  5 Chip off the scale formed using a chipping hammer and clean the weld using a wire brush.
