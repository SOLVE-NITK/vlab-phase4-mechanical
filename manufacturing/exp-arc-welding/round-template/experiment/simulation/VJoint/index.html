Arc Welding (V - Butt Joint)

Objective:

To Prepare a v joint using arc welding process.

Apparatus Used:

Wire Brush, Grinding Machine, Protective Equipments, Welding Transformer, Electrodes, Tong, Chipping Hammer, Mild steel flat of 60x45x10mm etc.

STEP 1Clean the given workpiece thoroughly, i.e. remove the rust, scales and form v cutting in the edges.

STEP 2Align the workpieces properly to form a vjoint.

STEP 3Turn your pieces over.

STEP 4Adjust the current in machine.

STEP 5Make tack welds.To make a tack weld, strike an arc and let it sit for a few seconds.

STEP 6Flip your metal over to be welded.

STEP 7Strike the arc and move the electrode to the other end uniformly.

STEP 8 Clean the weld with a hammer and wire brush.
