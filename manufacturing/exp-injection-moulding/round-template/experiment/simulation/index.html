INJECTION MOULDING

Objective:

To study and observe Injection moulding techniques through demonstration.


Apparatus used:

Injection moulding Machine, Protective Equipments, Plastic granules etc.

STEP  1 Put the plastic granules into the machine.

STEP  2  Set up the machine and close the mould.

STEP  3 Move the screw towards the mould.The heating coil will melt the plastic and it is injected into the mould.

STEP  4  Open the mould once the molten plastic is cooled.

STEP  5  Take out the final product from the machine.
