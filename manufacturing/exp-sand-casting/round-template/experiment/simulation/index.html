CASTING FOR A SOLID PATTERN

Objective:

To prepare a casting for the solid pattern using sand moulding process.


Apparatus used:

Moulding sand, Pattern, Moulding Boxes, Runner, Riser etc.

STEP  1  Set the inverted drag box on a clean floor and place the pattern inside the box.

STEP  2  Pour the sand into the box and level it off.

STEP  3  Turn over the packed drag box and place the cope box over it.

STEP  4  Place the second half of the pattern exactly over the first half.

STEP  5  Fill the sand and level it off.

STEP  6  Open the two boxes.

STEP  7  Remove the pattern from the box.

STEP  8  Put the boxes back into position and clamp it.

STEP  9  Setup gate and sprue.

STEP  10  Pouring molten metal.

STEP  11  Take cast outside.

STEP  12  Use the grinder for final finishing.
