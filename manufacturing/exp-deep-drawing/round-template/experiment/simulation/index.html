DEEP DRAWING

Objective:

To make a symmetrical cup of circular cross section using a draw tool and perform drawing operation.


Apparatus used:

Aluminium sheet of required size, Hydraulic press etc.

STEP  1  Set the blank at correct position.

STEP  2  Set the blank holder above the blank.

STEP  3 Apply force on the press to deep draw the Blank.

STEP  4  Release the hydraulic press.

STEP  5  Remove the blank holder.

STEP  6  Remove the finished product from deep drawing assembly.
