Deep Drawing

Objective:

To make a symmetrical cup of circular cross section using a draw tool and perform drawing operation.

Apparatus Used:

Aluminium sheet of required size, Hydraulic press etc.

STEP 1Set the blank at correct position.

STEP 2Set the blank holder above the blank.

STEP 3Apply force on the press to deep draw the Blank.

STEP 4Release the hydraulic press.

STEP 5Remove the blank holder.

STEP 6 Remove the finished product from deep drawing assembly.
