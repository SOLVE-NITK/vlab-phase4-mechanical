BLOW MOULDING

Objective:

To study and observe blow moulding techniques through demonstration.

Apparatus Used:

Blow moulding Machine, Protective Equipments, Plastic granules etc.

STEP 1Put the plastic granules into the machine.

STEP 2Set up the machine by placing the mould at proper position.

STEP 3Turn on the machine.The screw will move towards the mould and the heating coil will melt the plastic which is injected into the mould.

STEP 4Allow the air to enter into plastic parison,thereby stretching it across the interior walls of the mold cavity.

STEP 5Remove the mould.

STEP 6Take out the final product from the machine.
